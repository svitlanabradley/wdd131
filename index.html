Ukraine, the largest country in Europe by area, boasts stunning landscapes, rich culture, and a deep history. Known for its golden wheat fields, sunflower expanses, and the winding Dnipro River, its natural beauty complements its lively cities. Kyiv, the capital, blends ancient landmarks with modern architecture, while Lviv charms visitors with cobblestone streets and baroque buildings. In the west, the Carpathian Mountains offer breathtaking scenery, outdoor adventures, and traditional crafts.
